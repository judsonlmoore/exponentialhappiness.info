What Will You Get From This Book?

Exponential Happiness is an action-oriented travel guide for accomplishing goals in your life's journey toward the pursuit of Happiness.

Define your happiness

Only you can decide how to live your life best. Still, it is a process of discovery to uncover your true desires.

Identify your life goals

It's not easy discovering "what you want to be when you grow up." Together, this book and you will figure it out.

Create an actionable plan

Knowing what you want is one thing; learning how to get there is a whole other challenge. Achieve success with a plan.

Embrace failure as an opportunity

You will fail. Embrace it! The more you fail, the more you learn. The more you learn, the more you will succeed.

Live a life without regrets

When you do your best, you will still make mistakes. But that is no reason to feel trapped by regrets.

Confidence to say "yes"

Opportunities are all around you. Showing up is not always easy and takes practice like anything else.

About The Author

Judson L Moore is a product management professional with more than 20-years of work experience. His career has taken him across the globe to fill various roles in a breadth of industries. Judson's passions include applying technology and innovation to solving traditional problems, sharing his experiences through writing and interviews, and committing to making his community a better place for all who live in it.

Among his accomplishments, some highlights include improving the landing experience of +1 Billion users of trivago.com, building a publishing platform for journalists in Central Asia, and being the White Stripes album photographer. All of which also makes Judson a great storyteller.

When he is not on the job, Judson spends time engaging with his community through his Rotary Club, traveling as much as possible, blogging about his experiences, playing the drums, and cycling.

Judson's 5-year goals include writing another book, speaking at conferences, and acquiring permanent residency in Germany.
